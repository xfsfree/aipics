Refund Policy

Digital Products Policy

Due to the digital nature of our AI-generated artwork, we generally do not offer refunds once the product has been delivered.

Exceptions

Refunds may be considered in the following cases:

How to Request a Refund

Email us at support@aiartshop.com within 7 days of purchase with your order details and reason for the refund request.

Processing Refunds

Approved refunds will be processed within 14 business days to the original payment method.
